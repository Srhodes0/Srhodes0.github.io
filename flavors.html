Classic New York

A timeless indulgence. Our Classic New York Cheesecake is smooth, rich, and unapologetically decadent — crafted with velvety cream cheese, fresh eggs, and a hint of vanilla atop a buttery graham cracker crust. It's the kind of cheesecake that doesn’t need toppings to impress — simple, elegant, unforgettable.

Strawberry Swirl

A dreamy twist on tradition. This velvety cheesecake is ribboned with sweet strawberry puree, creating a perfect balance of tangy and fruity in every bite. Set on a golden graham crust and baked to perfection, it's as stunning to look at as it is satisfying to devour.

Oreo Crunch

Cookies & cream lovers, this one’s for you. Our Oreo Crunch Cheesecake is loaded with crushed Oreo cookies, both inside the creamy filling and generously piled on top. Every slice delivers a heavenly mix of chocolate crunch and silky cheesecake that’s impossible to resist.
